Call For Papers

The 1st Workshop on Evaluation and Comparison for NLP systems (Eval4NLP), co-located at the 2020 Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing (EMNLP2020), invites papers of a theoretical or experimental nature describing recent advances in system evaluation and comparison in NLP, particularly for text generation.

Important Dates (Tentative)

All deadlines are are 11:59 PM GMT -12 (anywhere-on-earth time)

Deadline for submission: July 15, 2020
Notification of acceptance: August 15, 2020
Deadline for camera-ready version: August 31, 2020
Workshop Date: December 11, 2020

Submission Guidelines:

Authors should submit a long paper of up to 8 pages, with up to 2 additional pages for references, or a short paper of up to 4 pages, with up to 2 additional pages for references, following the EMNLP 2020 formatting requirements. The reported research should be substantially original. Accepted papers will be presented as posters. Reviewing will be double-blind, and thus no author information should be included in the papers; self-reference that identifies the authors should be avoided or anonymized. Accepted papers will appear in the workshop proceedings.
